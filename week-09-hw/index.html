Coffee is Life!

I didn't start drinking coffee really until I came to college and found the need for it. Before coming to college coffee was just a rare occasion type of drink. It quickly became an everyday drink when you need to stay awake to go to class, finish homework and study all in the same day. I am now a coffee lover through and through and I wouldn't have it any other way! I made this website to simply discuss my love for the beverage.
